# Project Overview - Cognitive Dissonance

**Version**: Definitive 3.0 (February 2026)
**Core Vision**: A haunting, intimate 3D experience where you physically hold a fragile glass AI mind together as its own thoughts try to escape. The machine is just glass — and you are the only one keeping it from shattering.

**High-Level Concept**
- You interact with a heavy industrial platter that serves as the interface to a disembodied AI.
- The AI is represented as a single large transparent glass sphere in the center, containing a living celestial nebula shader (GLSL, registered in `Effect.ShadersStore`) that degrades from calm blue to violent red as tension rises.
- Corruption patterns (colored tendrils via SolidParticleSystem) escape from the sphere center toward the rim.
- Your only action is to hold matching colored keycaps on the platter to pull the patterns back.
- Missed patterns spawn enemies (ProceduralMorphSystem with 7 Yuka AI traits) that try to shatter the sphere.
- The game is endless with logarithmic escalation — you play until the sphere inevitably shatters ("COGNITION SHATTERED").

**Player Progression**
- No traditional levels or win state — survival is the goal.
- Buried seed (mulberry32 PRNG) generates unique "dreams" each run (different pattern behaviors, enemy waves, visual signatures, audio evolution).
- Tension rises logarithmically with missed patterns and enemy hits.
- Coherence (internal metric) can be boosted by successful stabilization, giving brief moments of clarity (sphere calms, beautiful blue pulse).
- High score = levels survived + peak coherence.

**How Players Lose**
- Sphere reaches max tension (0.999) → shatters in 64 procedural shards.
- Screen fills with intensified static and post-process corruption.
- Symmetric ending: "COGNITION SHATTERED" fades in on a diegetic plane (mirroring opening "COGNITIVE DISSONANCE").
- Platter keeps rotating, PLAY keycap re-emerges through garage-door slit for restart ritual with new buried seed.

**Technical Stack (v3.0 — Version Locked)**
- **Reactylon Native 3.5.4** + **Babylon.js 8.51+** for cross-platform 3D rendering and interaction
- **Expo SDK 55** + **Metro** as the universal bundler for web, Android, and iOS
- **React 19.2+** + **React Native 0.83** for the component layer
- **Miniplex 2.0 ECS** for entity management (`world.with()`, `world.add()`)
- **GSAP 3.13+** for advanced mechanical animations (CustomEase, MotionPath, Flip, stagger, timeline)
- **Tone.js** for spatial ambient score that evolves with tension
- **expo-audio v14+** for native audio bridge (Expo SDK 55 AudioContext bridging)
- **expo-haptics** for native haptic feedback
- **Zustand 5.0** for state management (game-store, seed-store, input-store)
- Buried seed (mulberry32 PRNG) for all procedural generation — no `Math.random()` outside factory
- **Havok WASM** physics for keycap springs (stiffness 800, damping 40), lever hinge resistance, platter torque
- Post-process corruption (`DefaultRenderingPipeline`), `SolidParticleSystem` for tendrils, diegetic GUI
- **babel-plugin-reactylon** for JSX tree-shaking and auto-lifecycle management
- **Build target**: <5 MB gzipped via tree-shakable `@babylonjs/core` subpath imports

**Platform Support**
- **Web**: WebGPU (Chrome 113+) with WebGL2 fallback
- **iOS**: Babylon Native via Metal (iPhone 12+ / A14+)
- **Android**: Babylon Native via Vulkan (Snapdragon 888+ with 6 GB RAM)
- **AR Glasses**: WebXR immersive-ar with 26-joint hand tracking
- **AR Phone**: Tap-to-place via WebXR hit-test, touch controls

**Thematic Identity**
- Intimate, hopeless, mechanical, beautiful tragedy.
- No HUD, no score display — everything is diegetic.
- The player is not a hero; they are a caretaker witnessing an inevitable breakdown.

**Replay Value**
- Every run is a different "dream" generated by the buried seed.
- 4 Dream archetypes: PlatterRotation, LeverTension, KeySequence, CrystallineCubeBoss.
- Procedural variety in patterns, enemies, audio, and visuals ensures no two games feel the same.
